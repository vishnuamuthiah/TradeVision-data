Support

We aim to respond within 48 hours.

Questions, bug reports, or a screenshot that didn’t import correctly? We’re happy to help.

Contact us

Email optionstradervision@gmail.com, or use the Support button on the App’s launch screen — it pre-fills a message and lets you attach screenshots.

What to include

For import problems, attaching the screenshot you tried to import (with any account details cropped out) helps us fix the parser quickly. For chart questions, the symbol, strategy, and expiration shown on screen are usually enough.
