Privacy Policy

Effective July 1, 2026

Your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains how TradeVision Edge (the “App”) handles information. In short: the App is built to work entirely on your device, and we do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information.

No Account, No Personal Data

You do not need to create an account to use the App, and the App does not ask for your name, email, or other personal identifiers. We do not collect personal information about you.

On-Device Processing

When you import a screenshot, the image is analyzed on your device using Apple’s on-device text recognition. Screenshots and the trade details read from them are processed locally and are not uploaded to us or to any third party. Trades you enter or import stay on your device.

No Tracking or Analytics

The App does not include third-party advertising, analytics, tracking, or marketing SDKs. We do not track your activity across other apps or websites.

No Network Transmission

The App’s features operate offline and do not send your data over the internet to us.

Photos Access

If you choose to import a screenshot, the App requests access only to the specific photo you select through the standard iOS photo picker. It uses that access solely to read the image you pick and does not browse or upload your photo library.

Sharing Between the App and Its Extension

If you use the share extension to send a screenshot into the App, the data is passed locally between the extension and the App on your device (via a shared app group) and is not transmitted externally.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date and, where appropriate, prompting you within the App.
